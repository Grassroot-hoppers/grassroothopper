# Citizen Science, Commons & AI for Social Good

## What it is

A constellation of European projects at the intersection of AI, citizen participation, and the public interest. Some are think tanks shaping policy around "Public AI." Others are grassroots initiatives putting AI tools directly in citizens' hands to tackle local challenges — climate, health, disinformation, democratic governance. What connects them: AI should serve communities, not extract from them.

## What Grassroots Hopper learns from it

**"Public AI" is a policy framework we can align with.** Open Future's advocacy for European Data Commons and public-interest AI procurement gives Grassroots Hopper a policy language. When pitching to municipalities or applying for grants, framing cooperative-owned tools as "Public AI" positions the initiative inside an existing policy conversation.

**Citizen science is a participation model, not just a research method.** Scivil's amai! project and Impetus4cs show that non-technical citizens can meaningfully participate in building AI solutions for their communities. This directly mirrors Grassroots Hopper's thesis — the barrier is falling, regular people can build.

**The social economy–tech bridge already exists.** Social Good Accelerator EU explicitly federates tech and civil society organizations. They advocate for algorithms under creative commons. This is a natural ally for the initiative — same values, complementary focus.

**AI governance needs to be co-created, not imposed.** AI4Citizens and ITHACA prove that regional governments are actively looking for ways to involve communities in AI governance. Grassroots Hopper cooperatives could position themselves as the local partners these programs need.

**Disinformation tools are a potential product vertical.** The TITAN project builds open coaching algorithms for citizens to identify and debunk disinformation. This could inspire a future Grassroots Hopper product — community-owned fact-checking and media literacy tools.

## Projects

### Open Future
European think tank advocating for "Public AI" and a European Data Commons. Aims to align AI procurement with public interest, open-source values, and climate goals.
- https://openfuture.eu

### Social Good Accelerator EU
Collective federating tech and civil society to build AI for social justice and the climate crisis. Advocates for algorithms released under creative commons.
- https://socialgoodaccelerator.eu

### Scivil & the amai! Project
Belgian knowledge center for citizen science. Involves citizens directly in developing AI solutions for local challenges — climate, health, mobility.
- https://www.scivil.be

### Impetus4cs
EU initiative funding citizen science projects. Recently awarded grassroots teams using open-source AI chatbots to gather lived experiences for local environmental policy.
- https://impetus4cs.eu

### AI4Citizens
Interreg Europe project helping regional governments co-create ethical AI governance frameworks alongside their communities.
- https://www.interregeurope.eu/ai4citizens

### ITHACA Project
European initiative using AI to enhance civic participation, ensuring platforms embed human rights and democratic values by design.
- https://www.ithaca-project.eu

### TITAN Project
EU-funded research project equipping citizens with AI-driven open coaching algorithms to identify, investigate, and debunk online disinformation.
- https://cordis.europa.eu/project/id/101070658
