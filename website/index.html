02 — THE THESIS

The technical barrier is gone.

AI-assisted development, open-source toolkits, and cooperative infrastructure are converging. Within the next few years, anyone who cares enough will be able to build real alternatives to the platforms they're stuck on. They'll search for a movement to join. Grassroot Hopper is that flag.

03 — THE BLUEPRINT

What Rob Hopkins did for energy, we do for technology.

Transition Towns: 1,400+ communities in 50+ countries. No corporation. No central authority. Just a name, a handbook, and passionate local leaders. Grassroot Hopper follows the same playbook — for digital infrastructure. Your community can own its own tools.

04 — THE IDENTITY

A person. A movement. Products.

A Grassroot Hopper is a person

Someone who blends technology with the actual pleasure of living a full life inside communities.

The Grassroot Hoppers are a movement

Community builders spreading city by city. The Transition Towns of the digital age.

The products are what they make

Open-source tools, cooperatively owned. Anyone in the movement can build anything.

06 — NO MONEY. OWNERSHIP.

Contributors don't get paid. They get ownership.
