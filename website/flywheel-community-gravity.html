Three streams flow into one view. Events from all your communities, the creative work of people inside those communities, and things members highlight for each other. That's it.

No algorithm decides what you see. Your communities decide. You mute what you don't care about. You discover what's left. The calendar becomes the single source of truth for your creative life.

The more communities you join, the richer your view becomes. And every community you join was entered in person — QR code, spoken password, real place. So everything you see comes from people you've actually met.

A calendar designed for you — by the communities you chose to be part of.

Not curated by an algorithm. Not sold to advertisers. Built from the communities you physically walked into, the people you actually know, and the things they think you should see.
